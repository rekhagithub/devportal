How it works

1. See what we have to offer and what works best for you.

2. Choose a plan that suits your needs.

3. Continuously integrate new APIs to fit what you need.

Create an Account

  • Register here and your information will be processed by our admin team.

  • Once we have reviewed your information you will receive an email with the appropriate credentials to log into the portal.

  • You'll receive an email link to validate your account and create a password.

Register Your Applications

  • APIs have simple plug and play capabilities by themselves or if needed then a cluster can be made in order to retrieve the information that is needed.
  • You can save the clusters that suit your needs in order to easily call them whenever you want in a quick and easy manner.

Get Access to APIs

  • Before you request access to specific APIs, first you need to determine what you need.

  • Once you know what you need the APIs for and which APIs fit those needs then you can request access to use specific APIs.

  • Upon gaining access you can begin to test and implement the APIs in your everyday use.

Try it out in the Sandbox

  • Use different APIs in the Sandbox in order to see how the APIs can be integrated together or how they work by themselves.

  • This feature can be used by beginners or by people who have more experience with working with APIs.

Go Live

Once the API is functional in the test environment then it is time to Go Live with the API and use it for.
